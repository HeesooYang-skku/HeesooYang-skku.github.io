About Me

A practice that moves between artistic research, visual language, and critical reflection.

I study Fine Art at Sungkyunkwan University and work across visual media, research, and writing. I am interested in the relationship between art, memory, technology, and the systems that frame how artworks are introduced to audiences.

Current Interest

Recently, I have been exploring how contemporary art exhibitions use repeated words and concepts in their titles, and how data visualization can reveal patterns in artistic and institutional language.
